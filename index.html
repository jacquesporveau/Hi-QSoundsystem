Hi-Q Soundsystem

West coast dub done live

Drawing from decades of collective experience, a classically trained dub instrumentalist and urban DJ collaborate as Hi-Q Soundsystem. With two high powered audio stations they perform irie originals and on-the-fly versions.

No two shows will ever be the same

Blending classic instruments with modern technology gives this duo the best of both worlds. Ian uses a wide array of instruments including: the trumpet, organ, melodica, synth and the bass guitar. On the other side of things we have Quinn holding down the back end of things via live remixing and dubbing.

A westcoast festival favorite

Having played numerous high profile BC festivals such as Rifflandia, Tall Tree, and Diversity; Hi-Q Soundsystem is quickly becoming a staple in western Canada's thriving festival culture.
